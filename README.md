# University of California, Santa Barbara (ucsb)

University of California, Santa Barbara (UCSB) is a public land-grant research university in the University of California system, ranked #79 in the QS World University Rankings 2025. UCSB runs a formal API Developer Portal at [developer.ucsb.edu](https://developer.ucsb.edu/) exposing campus enterprise APIs across Academics, Administration, Dining, Employees, Housing, and Students — most of which are gated behind registration and manual account approval. The Library also operates the Alexandria Digital Research Library (ADRL) institutional repository.

## APIs

- **UCSB API Developer Portal** — central portal for discovery, subscription, and access requests. [Docs](https://developer.ucsb.edu/docs/getting-started)
- **UCSB Academics APIs** — Academic Curriculums, Graduate Programs, Quarter Calendar, CLAS Schedules, Events. [Docs](https://developer.ucsb.edu/content/academic-curriculums)
- **UCSB Students APIs** — Student Courses, Registrations, Rosters, Schedules, Academic Programs, Photos. [Docs](https://developer.ucsb.edu/content/student-courses)
- **UCSB Administration APIs** — financials, chartfield/COA, ClassCode lookup, access cards. [Docs](https://developer.ucsb.edu/apis/all)
- **UCSB Dining APIs** — Dining Commons, Menu, Cams, Patrons, Meal Plan Rates. [Docs](https://developer.ucsb.edu/apis/all)
- **UCSB Housing APIs** — Contract Status, Housing Assignments. [Docs](https://developer.ucsb.edu/apis/all)
- **UCSB Employees APIs** — Employee Job and identity mapping. [Docs](https://developer.ucsb.edu/apis/all)
- **Alexandria Digital Research Library (ADRL)** — Samvera/Fedora institutional repository. [About](https://alexandria.ucsb.edu/welcome/about)
